# Clinical Case Scenario Generator

Generate realistic clinical case scenarios for medical education and assessment. Customizable by specialty, complexity level, and learning objectives.

## Quick Install

```bash
npx skills add gitjfmd/open-medical-skills --skill medical-case-generator
```

## What It Does

- Generate realistic clinical case scenarios for medical education and assessment. Customizable by specialty, complexity level, and learning objectives.

## Clinical Use Cases

- Medical knowledge assessment and review
- Case-based learning and clinical reasoning
- Board exam preparation and practice

## Safety & Evidence

- **Safety Classification:** Safe — This skill provides informational support and does not directly influence clinical decisions.
- **Evidence Level:** Moderate — Supported by well-designed observational studies or expert consensus guidelines.

Prompt: "Generate a practice question on cardiac physiology for USMLE Step 1."
